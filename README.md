# Glossina-pallidipes-transcriptomics
This work entails the transcriptomic differential gene expression of field-collected G. pallidipes from Shimba Hills National Reserve in Kenya. The flies were immobilized and dissected to check the presence of m=immature and mature Trypanosome congolense parasites. The total RNA was isolated from head tissues of the control and infected flies.
